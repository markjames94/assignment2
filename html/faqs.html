FAQ's

How long should my workouts last?
Your workouts should last between 20 minutes and 1 hour, any time over that is putting yourself at risk of injury.

How many sets and reps should I do?
In most cases, 3 sets of 8 to 12 reps is recommended. For the heavier lifts for strength training, 5 sets of 5-6 reps is recommended.

How can I set up a good workout plan?
The best way to set up a workout that is suitable for you is to isolate the training for set days. For example, on Mondays, train the chest, Tuesdays, target the shoulders, Wednesday for the legs, Thursday, train the back, on Fridays target the arms and the core. Use the weekend as a rest, or alternatively, have a rest day midweek and a rest day on the weekend.

Should I be doing cardio as well as weight training?
Cardio helps to remove body fat, if you have access to a treadmill or want to go out for a run, a 10-20 minute run or jog would be helpful to get the blood flowing. Alternatively, you could have a cardio day where your main aim is to get fitter and remove excess body fat.
