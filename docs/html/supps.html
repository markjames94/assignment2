Supplements

When you're training, supplements are always a big help. Different supplements do different things. For example; Whey Protein speeds up the muscle recovery process, Creatine increases muscle growth. Here are the best supplements to use.

Whey Protein - helps increase muscle recovery time.

Creatine - increases muscle growth when lifting.

BCAA - these are amino acids the can only be obtained through dietary sources.

Alpha Men/Alpha Woman - these are multi-vitamin tablets, the mens and ladies tablets are different from each other as men and women need different vitamins.

Thermopure - these are fat loss tablets. They are more effective when you're exercising and make you look leaner.

CLA - these are a source of healthy and naturally occuring Omega-6 fatty acids that are typically found in beef and cheese. CLA's provide a large amount of Conjugated Linoleic Acid.
